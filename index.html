💡 Game Rules
  • 🎯 Goal: First to 10,000 points wins
  • 🚀 Entry: Need 500+ points to get "on the board"
  • 💥 Farkle: No scoring dice = lose all pending points
  • 🏁 Hot Dice: Score with all 6 dice = roll again
  • 🔒 Lock & Roll: Lock scoring dice, roll the rest
  • 🏦 Banking: Bank points to secure them and end turn
💡 Pro Tip: Higher combinations override individual dice scoring. For example, three 1s = 1,000 points (not 300 points for 3 individual 1s).
